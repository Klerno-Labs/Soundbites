## ⚡ Quick Decision Matrix

### When to Use Interactive Add-On:
✅ You want high completion rates
✅ Simple quiz (5-10 yes/no or multiple choice)
✅ Don't need complex scoring logic
✅ Lead capture is main goal
✅ Have budget for ads ($20+/day)

### When to Use Website Quiz:
✅ Complex scoring algorithm needed
✅ Want full brand control
✅ Need detailed analytics
✅ Have strong organic TikTok presence
✅ Can drive free traffic (no ads)

### Best Approach: BOTH!
1. **Interactive Add-On** for paid ads (high conversion)
2. **Website Quiz** for organic traffic (full experience)
3. Track both in your Marketing tab
